## What is a good architecture?

A good architecture is one that meets the requirements:

- Asking what the requirements and constraints are
- Requirements should be
  - Verifiable
  - monitorable
  - Traceable
  - **feasible**

Examples

- 99% uptime
- users can do a task
- Meets ISO standard
